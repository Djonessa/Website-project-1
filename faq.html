Plaster Faqs

What is Venetian Plaster?
Venetian Plaster originated in Venice, Italy
and isa luxurious finish that has been used
since ancient times It's crafted by layering
plaster mixed with lime, marble dust
or other additives. This createsa smooth,
reflective surface resembling marble,
with a lovely satiny sheen.
