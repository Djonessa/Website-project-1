Our Story

Willkommen,
Founded in 2022 by Albert Owusu, our journey began with a passion for exquisite design and a dedication to exceptional craftsmanship.
In 2021 Albert completed his interior decorating apprenticeship in Germany before relocating to America. A year later, crafted from a love of design
and a vision to use his artisanal knowledge to transform ordinary walls into opulent masterpieces, Der Maler Painting was born.

Our philosophy

Crafting beauty, sustaining elegance.
At der maler, we believe that true luxury is found in the details. Our collections are a testament to this belief, featuring
the finest materials and finishes. From venetian plaster to bespoke wallpaper designs, each piece is a celebration of artistry
and a reflection of our commitment to quality.

Our team


Driven by creativity, united by vision.
Our team of designers, artisans, and visionaries are the heartbeat of Der Maler. With diverse backgrounds and a shared love of design. We collaborate to bring your dream spaces to life.
Let's create beauty together, reach out, share your vision, and let us make it a reality.
